⚠ What this backtest cannot tell you
Sample size is dangerously small
4 halving cycles. 4 multi-signal events. 8 MVRV sub-1.5 entries. "100% hit rate on 4 events" — a coin flip has a 6.25% chance of heads 4× in a row. We have 4 data points.
Market structure has fundamentally changed
2013 Bitcoin was a $1B asset with zero institutions. 2026 is a $1.4T asset with ETFs, corporate treasuries, sovereign interest. Patterns from retail-driven bear cycles may not repeat identically.
4th halving cycle is already anomalous
Weakest 12m post-halving return on record (+31% vs avg +540%). ATH came 5.5m post-halving instead of 12–18m. Historical halving pattern expectations for 2024 cycle require caution.
ETF signals have zero backtest history
Spot ETFs launched January 2024. One data point. We do not know how ETF holders behave during a sustained 60%+ drawdown — will they hold like LTHs or redeem like traditional fund investors?
Survivorship bias in source data
The signal dates here come from analyst reports written looking backward. We selected events that "worked." We cannot know how many sub-threshold signals did NOT precede rallies — those don't get published.
✓ What this backtest can tell you
Historical context for current positioning
At MVRV 1.43, BTC is in the accumulation zone — the same zone as Nov 2018, Mar 2020, and Nov 2022 before major recoveries. This is meaningfully different from neutral/mid-cycle. The 7/10 score reflects genuine on-chain value, not just timing.
What to expect if signals do trigger
If BTC falls to $50–65k triggering MVRV sub-1.5 and multi-signal convergence, historical data gives you confidence that a 12m hold from that entry has returned 200–500%+ every single time it occurred.
Position sizing reality check
Even the best historical entries saw 20–60% drawdowns. If a 40% drop would force you to sell, your position is too large — regardless of signal strength.
12-month is the minimum viable horizon
90-day variance is enormous and misleading. 12-month is where the historical signal edge actually shows up. If you can't hold 12+ months, the backtest edge doesn't apply to you.
