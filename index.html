Why this card exists: Your other metrics (MVRV, realized price, LTH supply) tell you where you are in the cycle. This card tells you how fast the tide is moving. Regime changes in whale accumulation have historically led price moves by 3–6 weeks — this is your early-warning system.

Why smoothed, not weekly: Raw weekly whale netflow is noisy — a single ETF rebalance can swing the number ±20k BTC with zero conviction change. Comparing the 4-week smoothed average against the 12-week baseline strips out that noise and surfaces only genuine regime shifts.

⚠ Important caveat: "Whales" in CryptoQuant data = wallets holding 100–10,000 BTC. This bucket includes ETF custody wallets (BlackRock, Fidelity), OTC desks, and corporate treasuries — not just conviction hodlers. The Accumulation Trend Score from Glassnode is a cleaner single metric because it weights by wallet age and behavior, not just size.
Sources: CryptoQuant (whale netflow, wallets 100–10k BTC) · Glassnode Studio free tier (Accumulation Trend Score, 0–1 scale) · Bitbo (LTH supply). The 4wk and 12wk averages are computed weekly from CryptoQuant's Whale Ratio chart. If precise values are gated, estimates from color-band readings are used — rough precision is fine for regime detection since thresholds are wide (±15%).
